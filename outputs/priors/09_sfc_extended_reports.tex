	% ============================================================
	\section{Introduction}
	% ============================================================
	
	This report compiles all graphical outputs from the extended priors module 
	(\texttt{09\_sfc\_extended\_reports\_v2.R}).  
	It is structured by asset and includes:
	
	\begin{itemize}
		\item Histograms of $\delta_t$ and $z_t$
		\item Kernel densities of $\delta_t$ and $z_t$ by historical window
		\item Time series of $\delta_t$ and $z_t$ with p10--p90 prior bands
		\item Time series of $q_t = K^g / K^n$ with prior bands
		\item The $\delta$--$z$ plane with outlier classification
	\end{itemize}
	
	All plots are drawn directly from harmonized and SFC-complete data, without reconstruction steps.
	
	A concise narrative can be added after you inspect the figures.
	
	
	% ============================================================
	\section{How to Read This Report}
	% ============================================================
	
	Each asset subsection follows a consistent pattern:
	
	\begin{enumerate}
		\item \textbf{Distribution of implicit rates}  
		Histograms show the raw shape of $\delta_t$ and $z_t$.  
		Densities by historical regime show shifts in volatility, persistence, and structural breaks.
		
		\item \textbf{Time-path with Prior Bands}  
		Red dashed lines indicate p10–p90 ranges calibrated on 1950–1994 (high-quality window).
		
		\item \textbf{$q_t$ ratio path}  
		This is a structural diagnostic of the relationship between gross and net stock valuation.
		
		\item \textbf{Outlier map in $\delta$–$z$ space}  
		This identifies years where implicit retirement and depreciation jointly deviate from priors.
	\end{enumerate}
	
	The entire report is designed for data triage prior to reconstruction.
